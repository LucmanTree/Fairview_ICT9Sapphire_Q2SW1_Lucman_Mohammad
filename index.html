Regular Show Main Characters

This show is one of the most wild shows of the early 2010s. It is my childhood so I am happy to introduce to you the protagonists of the story two cool bros Mordacai and Rigby.

Mordecai

Species: Blue Jay

Mordecai is a cool-headed responsible Blue Jay but beware Mordacai is known to cheat on his girlfriends.

Rigby

Species: Raccoon

Rigby is energetic and mischievous, A guy who lives for the excitement. He was once known as the immature stupid one of the two but he was able to redeem himself and graduate high school. A true showcase of the value of hard work and dedication
